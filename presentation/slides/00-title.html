Hi — I'm Sadaf, and this is my LLM capstone: ClimateClaimVerifier. It scans climate posts on Bluesky, pulls the published news evidence that's relevant to each one, and flags the posts that don't hold up.

The key idea: it never issues a true-or-false verdict and it never puts a credibility score on anything — it hands you the evidence and the signals, and you decide. I'll show why that one constraint drove every design choice in the system.

And it all runs on local models — no API bills.
