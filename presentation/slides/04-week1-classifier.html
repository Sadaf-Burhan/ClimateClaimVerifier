Week 1 is the front door: does this post contain a checkable factual claim, yes or no? I framed it as a classification task — the LLM-appropriate use — and built it on gemma2:2b via Ollama, with a fact-checker persona and a few-shot set covering claim, opinion, and commentary.

The detail that mattered: structured JSON output — no parse errors, and the reason field makes every decision auditable. And the recall-first idea is seeded right here — the gate's job is to catch claims, not to judge them.

This stage is still the front door today, but it evolved: I switched the model to qwen2.5:3b in Week 3 for recall and speed, and made the few-shot leak-free in Week 4. Same gate, better engine.
